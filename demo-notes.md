## ðŸ’¡ Demo Tips

1. **Show PHI Redaction**: After ingesting a note, toggle between "PHI-Redacted View" and "Raw View" to show how names, SSN, phone numbers, and addresses are automatically detected and masked.

2. **Ask Follow-up Questions**: Use the Clinical Copilot to ask questions that span multiple notes, like:
   - "Summarize all the events from the past week"
   - "What specialists has the patient seen recently?"
   - "Are there any medication changes I should be aware of?"

3. **Click Source Pills**: Show how answers are grounded in actual records by clicking the source citations.

4. **Multiple Notes**: Ingest 2-3 notes in sequence to demonstrate how the AI can connect information across multiple clinical documents.
